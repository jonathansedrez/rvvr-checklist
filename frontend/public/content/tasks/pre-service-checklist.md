# Pre-Service Checklist

A comprehensive guide to preparing for Sunday service. Work through each section before doors open.

## Audio Setup

- Verify the main mixing board is powered on and faders are at unity
- Check all stage boxes and snake connections
- Test each microphone channel (vocals, instruments, click track)
- Confirm monitor mixes match performer preferences from last rehearsal
- Set master limiter to protect speakers during high-energy moments

## Video & Projection

- Launch the presentation software and confirm slides are loaded
- Test the confidence monitors on stage (pastor and worship leader views)
- Ensure the HDMI hand-off from lyric software to live cameras is working
- Confirm stream encoder is receiving video signal

## Lighting

- Run a pre-show lighting check across all scenes
- Verify haze machine fluid level and warm-up time
- Confirm DMX universe assignments haven't shifted after last week's changes

## Communications

- Distribute radios and confirm channel assignments for all team leads
- Test IEM talkback with the worship leader and front-of-house
- Brief volunteers on any changes to today's order of service

---

> **Note:** All issues found during pre-service check should be logged in the team channel before 9:45 AM so they can be triaged before first service.
